Triple A Games

Battlefield 1 has revived the series for me and a lot of people I know. It is one of my top 5 games for 2016. Lets talk about the gamemodes.
the best ones are Conquest and War pidgeons. Conquest is a 32 vs 32 humongous battlefeild unless your on Suez. Tons of vehicles and around 4 to 7 points per map.
These points act similarly to points in domination. Every once in a while the points will give you a point and the first team to 1000 points wins.
In War Pidgeons you have to find a pidgeon on the map and write a letter. than you must let it go and not let anyone shoot it down.This game is great.
The gameplay flows well and the game keeps you hooked for hours. My final rating is 8/10

Overwatch is possibly the best game to release this year and is blizzards first big game since The Lost Vikings. With a growing cast of
23 heroes when im writing this. Each character is completely unique and plays differently. This game features 3 types of play. Payload wich most of
know. It also features capture in which one team defends and one attacks and last king of the hill. Know this game isn't complicated but works well
with what it has. It's very polished my worst complaint with the game though is it takes a while for them to add more content but ive put so many
hours into the game and I plan to spend so many more it's a great game and has a solid progression system with its lootboxes. I give this game a 9/10

Payday 2 was made in 2013 and was a great game. You play as a bank robber and try to rob people. The creators come out with frequent updates
but sadly you must buy most of them
